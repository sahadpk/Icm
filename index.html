Welcome! We love God!

Indian Christian Movement is a non political Charitable Organization formed for Religious Freedom, national unity, unity among Christians and for national developments.

Learn more

Mission

Our main mission is for religious freedom, national unity, unity among Christians, national developments and prompting the Indian Christians to the main stream of national developments.

Vission

The main vision of Indian Christian Movement is to unite the different factions of Christian community to a common goal of love each other.

Adv. K.V. SABU

SECRETARY GENERAL, ICM

Adv. Sri. K.V. Sabu is the founder Secretary General of Indian Christian Movement from its inception during 2000. Even today, he is continuing as the Secretary General of this Association. Mr.Sabu is a practicing lawyer at the Hon’ble High Court of Kerala. He is the General Secretary of Drisya Media Foundation, Global General Secretary of Global Malayalee Council, Vice President of MTVA, Kerala State. He is the Managing Committee Member of Jacobite Syrian Sabha, India & Middle East, Director of Manava Ashramam and National Vice President of Air Passengers Association. Mr. Sabu is also the State Executive Committee Member of BJP, Kerala, State Convenor of Election Cell, BJP, Kerala and also the National Executive Committee Member of BJP Minority Morcha. He is also holding the responsible post of several other District and local associations. He was the Additional Central Government Standing Counsel before the Hon’ble High Court of Kerla from 1998 to 2004 and also standing counsel for BSNL, DRI, Housing Board and PWD.
